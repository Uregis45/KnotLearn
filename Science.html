Science

Explore and Understand the World

Living Things

Living things can grow, move, breathe, and reproduce.

Examples: Humans, animals, plants

Non-Living Things

Non-living things do not grow or breathe.

Examples: Stone, water, chair

States of Matter

Matter exists in three main states.

Solid: Ice

Liquid: Water

Gas: Air

Energy

Energy helps us do work and move.

Examples: Light energy, heat energy
