Mathematics

Learn Mathematics Step by Step

Addition

Addition means putting numbers together.

Example: 2 + 3 = 5

Subtraction

Subtraction means taking away.

Example: 7 − 4 = 3

Multiplication

Multiplication means repeated addition.

Example: 3 × 4 = 12

Division

Division means sharing equally.

Example: 12 ÷ 3 = 4
